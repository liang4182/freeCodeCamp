Father of Information Theory

Main achievement

Shannon is known as a pioneer of the information age, ever since he demonstrated in his seminal paper “A Mathematical Theory of Communication” (1948) that information could be defined and measured as a scientific notion. The paper gave rise to “information theory”. He contributed significantly to its applications such as cryptography, artificial intelligence, telecommunications, and coding theory.
